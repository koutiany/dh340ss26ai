Comparing Language Models

In our course, students used LM Studio to compare different open-source language models:

  • Model Sizes: Comparing responses from models of varying parameter counts
  • Different Origins: Testing models developed by different organizations
  • Identical Prompts: Observing how the same input produces different outputs

Key Insight: These comparisons helped students understand that AI responses are not fixed facts but depend heavily on how models are trained, their size, and their specific design choices.
