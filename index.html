Demystifying Artificial Intelligence

Exploring artificial intelligence through digital humanities at Michigan State University

About This Course

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become increasingly common in our professional and private lives, yet students' understanding of basic knowledge of AI remains rather limited. This course demystifies AI through learning core concepts, visiting physical infrastructure, and hands-on projects.

Course Highlights

Hands-on Learning with LM Studio

Students used MSU's High Performance Computing Center's LM Studio to compare outputs of various open-source language models of different sizes and origins by asking identical prompts and observing how responses varied.

Critical Analysis

By comparing different text analysis approaches, students learned how preprocessing decisions and parameter choices shape topic modeling outcomes, demonstrating why transparency matters in scientific research.

Key Takeaways

This course demonstrates how emerging AI technologies are reshaping teaching and learning by offering new (and fun) ways for students to explore data and analysis, while also raising important questions about infrastructure and interpretive choices built into these tools.
