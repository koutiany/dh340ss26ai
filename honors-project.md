<section class="section">
    <h2>About the Project</h2>
    <p>
        This honors project explores the chatbots created by three major news publishers, 
        examining how AI-powered conversational interfaces are being integrated into journalism 
        and news delivery.
    </p>
</section>

<section class="section">
    <h2>Research Focus</h2>
    <div class="content-card">
        <h3>Project Overview</h3>
        <p>
            The student investigated how different news organizations have developed and deployed 
            AI chatbots for various purposes, including:
        </p>
        <ul>
            <li>News delivery and summarization</li>
            <li>Reader engagement and interaction</li>
            <li>Content recommendation</li>
            <li>Fact-checking assistance</li>
        </ul>
    </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
    <h2>News Publishers Studied</h2>
    <div class="two-column">
        <div class="content-card">
            <h3>Publisher 1</h3>
            <p>
                Analysis of their chatbot's approach to news delivery and interaction patterns.
            </p>
        </div>
        <div class="content-card">
            <h3>Publisher 2</h3>
            <p>
                Examination of how AI is used to enhance reader engagement and content discovery.
            </p>
        </div>
        <div class="content-card">
            <h3>Publisher 3</h3>
            <p>
                Investigation into their implementation and use cases for AI-powered conversations.
            </p>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
    <h2>Key Findings</h2>
    <div class="highlight-box">
        <h3>Emerging Trends</h3>
        <p>
            The project reveals how news organizations are experimenting with AI to remain 
            competitive and meet changing reader expectations in the digital age.
        </p>
    </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
