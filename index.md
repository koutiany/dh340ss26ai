    <h2>About This Course</h2>
    <p>

        AI through learning core concepts, visiting physical infrastructure, and hands-on projects.
    </p>

<section class="section">
    <h2>Course Highlights</h2>
    <div class="two-column">
        <div class="content-card">
            <h3>Hands-on Learning with LM Studio</h3>
            <p>
                Students used MSU's High Performance Computing Center's LM Studio to compare outputs of various 
                open-source language models of different sizes and origins by asking identical prompts and 
                observing how responses varied.
            </p>
        </div>
        <div class="content-card">
            <h3>Critical Analysis</h3>
            <p>
                By comparing different text analysis approaches, students learned how preprocessing decisions 
                and parameter choices shape topic modeling outcomes, demonstrating why transparency matters 
                in scientific research.
            </p>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>
