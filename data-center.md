    <h1>Data Center Visit</h1>
    <p>Grounding AI in physical reality</p>
</section>

<section class="section">
    <h2>Why Visit a Data Center?</h2>
    <p>
        AI often feels abstract—something that exists "in the cloud." To ground these concepts in physical reality, 
        our class visited MSU's on-campus data center. This experience helped students understand the tangible 
        infrastructure that powers the digital world.
    </p>
</section>

<section class="section">
    <h2>What We Experienced</h2>
    
    <div class="two-column">
        <div class="content-card">
            <h3>The Heat</h3>
            <p>
                One of the most striking aspects of the data center is the heat generated by computing infrastructure. 
                Students experienced firsthand how servers work to process data—and how much cooling is required 
                to maintain optimal operating temperatures.
            </p>
        </div>
        <div class="content-card">
            <h3>Hardware Lifecycles</h3>
            <p>
                We learned about the lifecycle of computing hardware—from installation to deprecation. 
                This raised important questions about e-waste, sustainability, and the environmental 
                impact of our digital infrastructure.
            </p>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
    <h2>Connecting AI to Infrastructure</h2>
    <p>
        The visit helped demystify AI by revealing what actually happens when we "use AI":
    </p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Physical Servers:</strong> AI models run on real hardware, not in an ethereal "cloud"</li>
        <li><strong>Energy Consumption:</strong> Training and running AI models requires significant electricity</li>
        <li><strong>Cooling Systems:</strong> Massive cooling infrastructure keeps servers from overheating</li>
        <li><strong>Maintenance:</strong> Hardware requires regular maintenance and eventual replacement</li>
    </ul>
</section>

<section class="section">
    <h2>Key Takeaways</h2>
    <div class="highlight-box">
        <h3>Environmental Considerations</h3>
        <p>
            The data center visit sparked important conversations about the environmental footprint of AI. 
            Questions like "How much energy does it take to train a large language model?" and 
            "What happens to old servers?" became central to our discussions.
        </p>
    </div>
</section>
